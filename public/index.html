Upwork AI Proposal Agent

Vollna alerts → AI scoring → Gemini proposals → Gmail drafts → Slack & Sheets

What it does

  • Reads Vollna job emails from Gmail
  • Scores jobs against your skills profile
  • Writes tailored proposals with Google Gemini
  • Saves Gmail drafts and logs to Google Sheets
  • Portfolio dashboard with analytics & send workflow

Stack

  • Python · Gmail API · Gemini · Google Sheets · Slack · Streamlit
